Getting Slack User ID:

  • Step 1
    •
    • To begin, make sure you have Slack opened, and that you're logged in. Once you do that, press the Continue button below.
  • Step 2
    •
    • Next, you will see some channel, doesn't matter which. What's important is you go to your profile. Refer to the image below to see where to press
    • Press here for the image if it's not clear on your display.
    • Once you press your profile picture, press "Profile" in the menu that shows up afterwards, right above "Preferences".
    • When you're done, press "Continue" below.
  • Step 3
    •
    • Now, you will see a side menu of sorts. Refer to the image below to see what to expect.
    • Press here for the image if it's not clear on your display.
    • Once you're sure you're in the right place, press the three, vertical dots next to "Set Status" near the botton right of the image above.
    • Once you press that, you're met with another menu. Reference the image below for that menu.
    • Press here for the image if it's not clear on your display.
    • Great, press the option called "Copy member ID"
    • Paste that back into the Google Form, where it asks you for your User ID, which is question 1.
